Quick Tips

Train tickets: Book on SBB.ch or Trenitalia. 1st class is worth it for the 4hr ride — more space, quieter, and the Alpine views are unreal.
Currency: Italy uses EUR, Switzerland uses CHF. Cards accepted almost everywhere in both countries.
Weather (early March): Milan 5–12°C, Geneva 1–9°C. Pack layers and a warm coat. Geneva will be noticeably colder.
Halal food: Milan has great halal options around Porta Venezia. In Geneva, check Carouge area and restaurants near the mosque (Petit-Saconnex).
Reservations: Book Langosteria, Les Armures, and Izumi in advance. Other spots are walk-in friendly.
